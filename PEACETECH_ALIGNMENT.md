# PeaceTech Alignment

## Primary Track

`Voice & accountability`

## One-line Business Case

Civic Access Navigator helps citizens turn confusing rights and civic information into timely, accountable public action.

## Why This Track Fits

The product is most effective as a `Voice & accountability` tool because it focuses on the gap between citizens and the institutions meant to serve them.

It promotes this theme by:

- translating rights and civic information into plain language
- helping citizens ask better questions before approaching institutions
- surfacing trusted public-interest sources instead of rumor or guesswork
- offering a quick path to escalation through chat and SOS actions
- creating a reusable accountability workflow that can expand from Kenya into other African contexts

## Product Boundaries

This prototype does not claim to replace emergency responders, lawyers, or community mediators.

It is designed to:

- orient users quickly
- guide them toward trusted information
- help them decide what accountable next step to take

## Documentation-only Concepts

The following concepts remain important to the project, but no longer appear as primary UI chips on the home screen:

- Language-aware
- Doctrines-aware
- Culturally local

They remain part of the design and expansion logic, but are now expressed in documentation and system behavior rather than as hero-level labels.
