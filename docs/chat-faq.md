# Chat FAQ

This document is the human-readable FAQ companion to the app's internal chat knowledge base.

## What is Civic Access Navigator?

Civic Access Navigator is a Kenya-first PeaceTech prototype that helps users move from understanding a right to taking a safer, incident-specific accountability step.

## Which PeaceTech track does CAN fit best?

CAN fits best in the Voice & Accountability track because it strengthens civic participation, supports incident reporting, and reduces the gap between citizens and institutions.

## What are the basic peace baselines for citizens in Kenya?

In the current prototype, the Kenya baseline combines rights protection, access to justice, participation, human dignity, non-discrimination, personal security, and the ability to seek remedy or trusted support without unnecessary risk.

## What is Safe Mode?

Safe Mode is for physical-risk situations. It suppresses location-first actions, resets visible chat history, and shifts the interface toward privacy-preserving use.

## What is Lite Mode?

Lite Mode is for low-bandwidth or constrained-device use. It reduces visual weight, leans on cached content, and prioritizes readability and continuity under unstable connectivity.

## How does CAN help a displaced user?

The prototype changes its workflow by scenario. For displaced users it prioritizes movement-safe guidance, temporary support options, lower-friction escalation, and later delivery of accountability actions when connectivity returns.
