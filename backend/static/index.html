One-line business case

Fast path to action

Civic Access Navigator helps citizens turn confusing rights information into timely, accountable civic action.

How the theme shows up

The tool helps people understand rights, prepare questions, and reach institutions with clearer, safer information.

Why it matters now

Conflict-affected communities often need fast, plain-language guidance before they can report harm or seek remedy.

Scale path

Kenya is the pilot, with the same accountability workflow ready to expand into the Sahel, DRC, Sudan, and beyond.
